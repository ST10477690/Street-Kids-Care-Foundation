Services(Core Offerings)

Basic Needs Support

  • Food programs (daily meals, food parcels)
  • Clothing donations (school uniforms, warm clothes, shoes)
  • Temporary shelter or referrals to safe housing

Education & Skills Training

  • Literacy and numeracy classes
  • School enrollment support (books, stationery, fees)
  • Vocational training (carpentry, sewing, gardening, IT, etc)
  • Life skills workshops (hygiene, financial Literacy, communication)

Counselling & Social Support

  • Trauma counselling and emotional support
  • Family reunification programs
  • Peer support groups and mentorship

Health & Wellness

  • Access to basic healthcare services (check-ups, immunisations)
  • Hygiene and sanitation programs (showers, clean clothes, toiletries)
  • Mental health support (therapy, referrals to specialists)

Products (to support fundraising/awareness)

Publications

  • Awareness bookklets, reports, or storybooks created with children's input
  • Annual reports and newletters (digital or print)
